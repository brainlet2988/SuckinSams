About Us

Meet Jordi

What’s poppin people! My names Richard Samuel Jordan, but you can call me Sam!

My humble beginnings all started back when I was a young lad playing in the Carolina swamp with my dog named “Jaeger.” I always had a passion and fascination for vacuums from a young age and a strong desire to conversate.

At the age of 35, I tragically lost my grandmother to an automobile wreck and fell into a dark pit of depression and anxiety; forcing me to take Lexapro. I also gained immense amounts of weight which forced me to get a weight loss surgery in Mexico, bringing me down to a perfectly healthy 450 pounds.

Fortunately, I discovered a video game named “Fallout” which quite literally saved my life. I joined the Air Force, graduated top of my class thanks to being a carried diamond, and earned the venerable reputation of “pistol grip Richard.”

Afterwards, I discovered a new game series called “Call of duty” which conversely, ruined my life. One day playing COD, I arranged myself in a tournament to 1v1 a British adversary named “Syndicate.”

Unfortunately, I succumbed to his superior gaming skill and IQ on a map called “Bog.” I am still recovering to this day.

Which brings you to the business you see today.

I became semi-retired and pursued my true passion of selling vacuum parts and vacuum part accessories. Together with my wife, Kelly, and my outstanding manager, Sean Ranklin, we proudly serve the Conway area for all your vacuum needs!

Come on down for some complimentary Pepsi and Chili while we take care of you.! We keep you suckin!
